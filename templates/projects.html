The Brown Log

- First Place @ LinkedIn Hack Day

(July 2014)



HTML5 CSS JavaScript MongoDB Angular Phone Gap
An elegant way for people to express their thoughts and ideas while doing something not so elegant. The Brown Log is a mobile app that allows you to quickly and easily share the epiphanies that come to you while on the loo. Snap a photo of the QR code in your bathroom stall and be directed to a forum where you can post text and doodles; or, browse the thoughts that have occurred in nearby bathrooms as well.
