aNYminute

@ HackNY

(September 2013)

Do you only have a certain amount of time each day to read articles from the New York Times? Our web application, aNYminute allows users to input a topic of their choice and the amount of time that they have to read articles. aNYminute will provide readers with articles they're interested in that they will be able to finish within their inputted timeframe.
